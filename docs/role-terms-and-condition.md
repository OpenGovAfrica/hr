### OGA Role Terms & Conditions Template
**Volunteer Graphic Designer (Stipend-Based)**

This document outlines the operational expectations and standards governing the engagement of the Volunteer Graphic Designer at OpenGOV Africa (OGA).

### 1. Reporting Structure

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Department | Marketing & Communications |
| Reports To | Marketing Lead / Program Lead |
| Work Mode | Remote |
| Weekly Commitment | 6–8 hours |

### 2. Scope of Work

The Designer will:

| Core Responsibilities |
|-----------------------|
| Design weekly social media graphics aligned with OGA branding |
| Create visuals for newsletters and blog publications |
| Maintain visual consistency across campaigns |
| Support event and open-source documentation visuals when required |
| Collaborate cross-functionally with content and technical teams |

### 3. Weekly Deliverables

| Deliverable | Quantity | Notes |
|-------------|----------|-------|
| Social Media Graphics | 3 per week | Static or carousel |
| Newsletter / Blog Visual | 1 per week | Header or supporting design |
| Submission Format | Weekly Batch | Designs for the upcoming week submitted together for review |

### 4. Turnaround & Revision Policy

- 6-hour turnaround once assigned  
- Weekly batch creation preferred  
- Maximum of 2 revision rounds per batch  
- Additional revisions/changes require prior approval  

### 5. Tools & Collaboration Infrastructure

| Category | Details |
|----------|----------|
| Primary Tool | Canva Pro (provided by OGA) - ask for access |
| Alternative Tool | Adobe Suite (if volunteer already has access) |
| File Storage | Google Drive - https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1kjAbBcm5LLnw-suZ-ultbdCMMIUBkeE0 |
| Task Management | GitHub Project Boards - https://github.com/orgs/OpenGovAfrica/projects/1 |
| Version Control | GitHub Account Required |

**GitHub Requirement Explanation:**  
Where applicable, design assets are stored in OGA repositories for documentation transparency, campaign archiving, and integration with open-source projects. Designers is required to upload assets directly to individual tasks/issues in the marketing repository to avoid duplication and ensure better team workflow - find marketing team issues/tasks here: https://github.com/OpenGovAfrica/marketing/issues.

**In Doubt?** 
Read your onboarding document here: https://docs.google.com/document/d/10aVJvP67CVmTCKSBgkYVqA5_QqH9_SlSRcKZcLFupuk?tab=t.rlihrtqzm9yi

### 6. Performance Evaluation

| Timeline | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Weekly | Review of submitted batch |
| Monthly | Informal performance check-in |
| End of Month 3 | Formal probation review |
| Post-Probation | Quarterly review (if extended) |

Evaluation Criteria:
- Timeliness  
- Quality & brand alignment  
- Responsiveness  
- Professional communication  
- Ability to meet turnaround standards  

### 7. Recognition & Professional Development

Upon satisfactory completion:

| Benefit |
|----------|
| Certificate of Completion |
| LinkedIn Recommendation |
| Portfolio Credit |
| Public Recognition across OGA platforms |

High-performing contributors may be considered for expanded leadership or paid creative roles where available.

### 8. Intellectual Property

| Policy | Details |
|--------|----------|
| Ownership | All designs created during engagement belong to OGA |
| Portfolio Use | Designer may showcase work with appropriate credit |
| Confidential Projects | Must not be shared without written request to marketing lead with founder in copy of email. Usage rights can only be given on approval from this email thread. |

### 9. Confidentiality & Conduct

The Designer agrees to:
- Adhere to OGA’s Code of Conduct  
- Protect internal documents and unpublished materials  
- Maintain professional standards in all communications  

### 10. Termination

| Condition | Terms |
|-----------|-------|
| Standard Termination | Either party may terminate with 2 weeks’ written notice |
| Immediate Termination | Misconduct, breach of confidentiality, violation of conduct policy, or repeated non-performance |

This document forms part of the Volunteer "Role/Graphic Designer" engagement agreement with OGA.
