# hr
Human Resource &amp; Volunteer Coordination

### HR Team – Tasks & Subtasks (OpenGov Africa)

The HR Team at OpenGov Africa focuses on **volunteer coordination, retention, impact tracking, and internal culture**. Since volunteers receive automated onboarding emails, the HR team primarily acts as **liaison officers** ensuring volunteers are properly integrated into teams and supported throughout their journey.

---

### 1. Volunteer Intake & Verification

**Objective:** Ensure every incoming volunteer from Idealist is properly documented and connected to the correct team.

**Tasks**

- Monitor Idealist volunteer applications
- Verify submitted onboarding forms
- Confirm volunteers have joined Discord
- Confirm volunteers understand the project they applied for

**Subtasks**

- Review onboarding form submissions daily
- Maintain a **Volunteer Intake Tracker (Google Sheets)**
- Verify that volunteers:
  - Joined Discord
  - Selected correct roles
  - Indicated preferred project
- Assign volunteers to the correct **Project Manager / Team Lead**
- Send confirmation messages introducing them to their team lead
- Flag incomplete onboarding forms for follow-up

---

### 2. Volunteer-Team Liaison Management

**Objective:** Act as the bridge between volunteers and project teams.

**Tasks**

- Introduce volunteers to project managers
- Ensure volunteers understand project expectations
- Monitor early-stage engagement

**Subtasks**

- Send introduction messages to PMs when a new volunteer joins
- Create a **handover note** summarizing:
  - Volunteer name
  - Skills
  - Time availability
  - Preferred project
- Track whether the PM has acknowledged the volunteer
- Follow up after **3–5 days** if volunteer has not been engaged
- Escalate inactive assignments to Operations Lead if necessary

---

### 3. Volunteer Hours Tracking

**Objective:** Track volunteer time contributions for transparency, reporting, and future funding applications.

**Tasks**

- Implement volunteer time tracking
- Maintain hours log
- Generate monthly contribution reports

**Subtasks**

- Create **Volunteer Hours Tracker (Google Sheets)**
- Request weekly time submissions from volunteers
- Validate hours with Project Managers
- Track hours by:
  - Volunteer
  - Project
  - Team
  - Task category
- Produce **monthly volunteer contribution reports**
- Identify top contributors

---

### 4. Impact Metrics & Reporting

**Objective:** Track the real impact of volunteer contributions across projects.

**Tasks**

- Define impact metrics
- Collect data from project teams
- Compile periodic reports

**Subtasks**

- Track metrics such as:
  - Volunteers active per month
  - Hours contributed
  - Projects supported
  - GitHub contributions
  - Research outputs
  - Community events supported
- Create a **Quarterly Volunteer Impact Report**
- Work with the Strategy team to translate metrics into **grant-ready impact data**

---

### 5. Volunteer Retention & Engagement

**Objective:** Maintain a motivated and active volunteer community.

**Tasks**

- Monitor volunteer engagement
- Recognize contributions
- Prevent volunteer drop-off

**Subtasks**

- Track inactive volunteers
- Send **monthly engagement check-ins**
- Organize recognition initiatives such as:
  - Volunteer of the Month
  - Project Contributor Highlights
- Maintain a **Volunteer Recognition Board**
- Support community managers with volunteer morale initiatives

---

### 6. Exit & Offboarding Management

**Objective:** Maintain clean records when volunteers leave or become inactive.

**Tasks**

- Track volunteer exits
- Conduct exit feedback

**Subtasks**

- Mark volunteers inactive after **30–60 days of inactivity**
- Conduct short **exit feedback surveys**
- Document reasons for leaving
- Maintain alumni list for future collaboration
- Archive volunteer records

---

### 7. HR Documentation & Records

**Objective:** Maintain organized documentation for operational continuity.

**Tasks**

- Maintain volunteer records
- Document HR processes

**Subtasks**

- Maintain a **Volunteer Directory**
- Document onboarding workflows
- Maintain HR guidelines for:
  - Volunteer expectations
  - Code of conduct
  - Participation guidelines
- Ensure all documentation is stored in **Google Drive**

---

### 8. Coordination with Operations & Strategy

**Objective:** Align volunteer management with organizational needs.

**Tasks**

- Report volunteer capacity to leadership
- Identify staffing gaps across teams

**Subtasks**

- Provide **monthly volunteer capacity reports**
- Flag teams needing additional volunteers
- Coordinate recruitment posts on Idealist when teams need support
- Share volunteer insights with Strategy team for program planning

---

### 9. HR Process Improvement

**Objective:** Continuously improve volunteer management systems.

**Tasks**

- Identify workflow inefficiencies
- Propose improvements

**Subtasks**

- Audit onboarding completion rates
- Analyze volunteer retention trends
- Suggest improvements to:
  - Onboarding forms
  - Team assignment process
  - Volunteer tracking systems
- Test new HR workflows before organization-wide rollout

---
