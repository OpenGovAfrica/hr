## General Onboarding

### 01. Why We’re Here
We exist to build a more transparent, accountable, and participatory Africa: one open dataset, one civic tool, one community at a time.  
OpenGov Africa is where governance meets open-source, data meets people, and passion meets purpose.

### 02. How We Work
Our vibe is collaborative, impact-driven, and unapologetically African.

We believe in:  
● Radical Transparency – Data and processes are open.  
● People Power – Our communities drive change.  
● Ownership – Everyone contributes. Everyone counts.  
● No Red Tape – We keep it lean, flexible, and fast.

### 04. First Steps Checklist
- Read this document carefully  
- Join our Discord server (https://discord.gg/Eswe4cvvMM)  
- Introduce yourself in #team-intros  
- Open the shared Google Drive folders  
- Follow us on socials  
- Each team has a lead + support circle. You’ll be guided to join based on your interest and skills.
